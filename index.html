Recipe 1

Mix together store-bought pesto and dijon mustard (about 50/50) in a bowl and spread generously over salmon filets. Bake on 350 degrees for 15 minutes (a little more if you like it more well done). Serve it with a side of couscous (follow box directions) mixed.

Recipe 2

Cut 3/4 pounds of filet mignon into smaller squares. Also chop up 1 head of cauliflower, 3 large carrots, 1 yellow onion and a few cloves of garlic. Brown the meat in a pan and then remove. Add onion, garlic, carrots and cauliflower into the pan with a little olive oil and cook until soft.
